Perks Available
to Students

This is a 13 weeks bootcamp. As a student, you'll have access to live sessions with your instructors. Currently, there are 3 available tracks to choose from. Each student will receive a certificate of completion upon satisfying the track's requirements. Other benefits to enjoy include:

Virtual Internships

As a member, you get to participate in free virtual internships from top companies that you can put on your profile.

Relevant Materials

You get to receive access to the source codes and other resources of the projects done except stated explicitly by your instructor.

Student Membership

You get to have a student membership badge which enables them to have access to exclusive info from the academy, even after the cohort ends.

Career Opportunities

All members will receive career support to position them for internships, junior or intermediate roles.

Mentorship

All members get to be assigned a mentor who will serve as a guide to helping you grow in your career.

Certificate

You get to receive a certificate to prove your acquired skills when you complete all requirements.
