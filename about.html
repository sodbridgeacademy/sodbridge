Vision statement

To be among the leading providers of tech skills in Africa with the provision of quality and affordable tech education.

Mission statement

To create communities of young and creative minds who are employable according to current tech trends and are independent enough to be their own boss.

Who we are

Sodbridge academy is a Nigerian EdTech company that focuses on providing tech skills to teens & youths. We offer a hybrid learning as we believe it's the most effective form of learning in the 21st century.

At Sodbridge, we also provide tech related services such as website design, web applications, mobile applications, machine learning solutions and more. We provide solutions to real world problems and bring your idea to reality via modern technology. This is done via collaborating with you to get your ideas and go through strategic steps to bring it to fruition. We'll always hold you by hand at every stage of the journey.

We're a team of top individuals who are dynamic, experienced and results driven. We are developers, engineers, analysts, designers and mentors. At Sodbridge, we are one and we look forward to having you join us.
